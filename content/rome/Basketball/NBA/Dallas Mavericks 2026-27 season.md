## Current state

The NBA's July 8 transaction record has Dallas receiving Santi Aldama, Marcus Sasser, and the rights to Tarik Biberovic while sending Khris Middleton, AJ Johnson, a protected 2030 first-round pick, and 2029 and 2033 second-round picks. The existing Dallas briefing already incorporates the transaction, so this page is the canonical current-season successor rather than a duplicate recap.

## Why it matters

The next questions are how Aldama changes frontcourt spacing, whether Sasser supplies enough secondary creation, and how health uncertainty around Kyrie Irving and Dereck Lively II changes minutes and lineup combinations.
