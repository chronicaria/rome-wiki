## Verified transaction delta

Dallas officially acquired Santi Aldama, Marcus Sasser, and Tarik Biberovic's draft rights on July 8 while sending out Khris Middleton and AJ Johnson with draft compensation. On July 10, the Mavericks' roster page still displayed Middleton and Johnson and did not display Aldama or Sasser.

The mismatch makes the roster page evidence of a publication lag, not evidence that the trade reversed. Minutes should not be allocated until the grid is reconciled and contract/two-way status is verified. The durable questions remain guard creation behind Kyrie Irving, the distribution of frontcourt minutes, and how much shooting can surround Cooper Flagg.

## Why it matters

A stale roster grid can contaminate both human depth charts and model inputs. Preserving the provenance conflict prevents false precision in the 2026-27 forecast and makes the next update mechanical: verify the corrected official roster, then assign role ranges rather than single-point minutes.
