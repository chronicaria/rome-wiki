## Current state

Duke announced Manny Diaz, Jeremiah Hasley, Nate Sheppard, and Luke Mergott as its representatives for the July 15–17 ACC Kickoff. The live schedule, its text-only representation, and Duke's May 27 kickoff-times release all list Tulane on September 5, so that date supersedes the June 24 release's September 3 reference.

The first four kickoff times are also now official: Tulane and Illinois at 3:30 p.m. ET, Stanford at 4 p.m. ET, and William & Mary at 3:30 p.m. ET. See [[Duke football 2026 schedule map]].
